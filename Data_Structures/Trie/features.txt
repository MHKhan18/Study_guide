


 **** Trie || Prefix Tree *****
#) Use cases:
- Word validation
- Autocomplete
- Spell checker
- Word games

#) Adept at:
- Finding all keys with a common Prefix
- Enumerating a dataset of strings in lexicographic order

#) Insertion:
- Time complexity : O(m), m is key length
- Space complexity: O(m)


#) Search:
- Time complexity: O(m)
- Space complexity: O(1)
